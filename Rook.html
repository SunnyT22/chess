How the rook moves

The rook can only move in straight lines; up, down, left, right..

The rook can capture an enemy piece in its way.

The rook can move any number of squares as long as there are no pieces in its way.

The rook can do a special move with the king called castling, when the king moves two squares and the rook moves next to the king.

The rook is like a queen but cannot move diagonally.

The rook is worth 5 points.
