How the king moves

The king can move in any direction; diagonally, up and down, sideways.

The king cannot move to a square attacked or controlled by another piece.

If another piece is attacking the king, it is called "check." When in check, you can capture the piece with one of your pieces, move your king to a safe square, or block the check.

The game ends when the king is in checkmate; the king is in check and cannot do anything.

The king can capture an enemy piece if it is in the squares that the king controls as long as the enemy piece isn't defened by another. piece.

The king is worth 0 points.
