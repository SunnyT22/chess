How the queen moves

The queen is the strongest and most powerful piece on the board.

The queen is like the king (the king can move one square in any direction) but is a long-range piece and can move further.

The queen can capture an enemy piece in its way.

The queen can move in any direction and can move any number of squares as long as there are no other pieces in its way.

The queen has the power of a rook and bishop combined.

The queen is worth 9 points.
